About PHD Communications

PHD has been installing and servicing business telephone systems in the Greater Boston and Northern New England markets since 1990 and is proudly named after it's three founders, Pat Dunn, Hugh Pringle and Dick Hamel.

We serve as the trusted, single source for our clients voice and data communications needs. From basic digital business phone systems, to the latest VOIP, cloud and Internet services, PHD can keep you in touch with your clients, and keep things running smoothly.

Service Area

We serve businesses based in New England, including support for their branch locations and remote users throughout the world. Emergency onsite service is available throughout New Hampshire, from Concord south through Manchester NH, and then down into Boston. From Northern and Central Mass we cover from Worcester, east to the North Shore, including the Salem and Portsmouth NH area.

Charitable Giving

At PHD, we are committed to being responsible, responsive and respected citizens in the communities in which we work and live. When we work with a corporate client to replace or upgrade their communications system, we are often able to offer the older equipment to local non profits at a greatly reduced cost. Our goal is to provide for those in need and assist those who are working to better their communities.
